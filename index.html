About

I'm Cameron. I graduated from Columbia with a math degree (magna cum laude, Phi Beta Kappa) and I'm currently doing my MS in Geophysics at Colorado School of Mines with a 4.0. I've published research, I code in Python regularly, and I've been doing math at a high level for a while.

I tutor because I'm good at explaining things clearly and I think a lot of students get stuck not because the material is hard, but because it wasn't explained well the first time. I'm not going to just give you answers — I want you to actually understand what's going on.

I also think there's a real problem with students leaning on AI tools to get through homework without learning anything. That works until it doesn't — and it usually stops working around exam time. I teach actual understanding.
